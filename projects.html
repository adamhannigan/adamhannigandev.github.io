Mobile Applications Project at University of Technology Sydney. Involved design documents, material design implementation, functional and user testing. Dirty Worker (Web MEAN stack)

Personal 4 month project to demonstrate Web development skills. Provided a crowd-sourcing alternative for teenagers to find local chores in the area. Written in Node, Angular 1, Express and MongoDB. Deployed on DigitalOcean and uses LetsEncrypt for HTTPS security. Mapped Australian POST database with Google Maps to get realtime geographical locations. Encrypted storage of data, ability to login with Facebook Oauth, NodeMailer for email capacity and much more! Fully responsive design.
